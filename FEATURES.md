# ShoopKeeper POS System - Feature Documentation
## Designed for African Retail Businesses

### üéØ Core Philosophy
This POS system is designed to solve the critical problem of shopkeepers forgetting transactions and losing track of inventory. It focuses on simplicity, offline-first operation, and features tailored for African retail environments.

---

## üìã Core Features

### 1. **Point of Sale (POS) Interface**
- **Quick Checkout**: Fast, intuitive sales interface
- **Barcode Scanning**: 
  - Camera-based barcode scanning (mobile phones)
  - USB barcode scanner support
  - Manual product search
- **Multiple Payment Methods**:
  - Cash
  - Mobile Money (M-Pesa, MTN Mobile Money, Orange Money, etc.)
  - Bank Transfer
  - Credit/Debit Card
  - Credit (with customer tracking)
- **Cart Management**:
  - Add/remove items
  - Quantity adjustment
  - Discount application
  - Price override (for authorized users)
- **Receipt Generation**: 
  - Print receipts
  - Email/SMS receipts
  - Digital receipt storage

### 2. **Inventory Management**
- **Product Management**:
  - Add/edit/delete products
  - Bulk import/export (CSV/Excel)
  - Product categories
  - Multiple units of measurement (pieces, kg, liters, etc.)
  - Product variants (size, color, etc.)
- **Stock Tracking**:
  - Real-time stock levels
  - Stock history/audit trail
  - Stock adjustments (damaged, expired, etc.)
  - Stock transfers between locations
- **Low Stock Alerts**:
  - Automatic notifications when stock is low
  - Reorder suggestions
  - Expiry date tracking
- **Barcode Management**:
  - Generate barcodes for products
  - Support multiple barcode formats
  - Custom barcode printing

### 3. **Customer Management**
- **Customer Database**:
  - Add/edit customer information
  - Customer purchase history
  - Customer contact details
- **Credit Management**:
  - Track customer credit
  - Payment reminders
  - Credit limit management
- **Customer Analytics**:
  - Most frequent customers
  - Customer lifetime value
  - Purchase patterns

### 4. **Sales & Reports**
- **Sales Dashboard**:
  - Today's sales summary
  - Daily/weekly/monthly reports
  - Sales trends and charts
- **Profit & Loss**:
  - Revenue tracking
  - Expense tracking
  - Profit margins
  - Cost of goods sold (COGS)
- **Product Performance**:
  - Best-selling products
  - Slow-moving items
  - Profit by product
- **Export Options**:
  - PDF reports
  - Excel/CSV export
  - Print reports

### 5. **Expense Management**
- **Expense Tracking**:
  - Record daily expenses
  - Categorize expenses (rent, utilities, salaries, etc.)
  - Receipt attachment
- **Expense Reports**:
  - Daily/monthly expense summaries
  - Expense trends
  - Budget vs actual

### 6. **Multi-Shop Management** (For Malls/Chains)
- **Multiple Shop Locations**:
  - Manage multiple shops from one account
  - Stock transfers between shops
  - Consolidated reporting
- **Role-Based Access**:
  - Owner/Admin
  - Manager
  - Cashier
  - Staff (view-only)

### 7. **Offline-First Architecture**
- **Offline Capability**:
  - Works without internet connection
  - Automatic sync when online
  - Conflict resolution
- **Data Backup**:
  - Automatic cloud backup
  - Local backup export
  - Data recovery options

### 8. **Multi-Language Support**
- **Languages** (Priority for Africa):
  - English
  - French
  - Swahili
  - Arabic
  - Portuguese
  - Local languages (expandable)

### 9. **Mobile-First Design**
- **Responsive Interface**:
  - Works on phones, tablets, desktops
  - Touch-friendly buttons
  - Large, readable fonts
- **Progressive Web App (PWA)**:
  - Install on phone home screen
  - Works like native app
  - Push notifications

### 10. **Advanced Features**
- **Analytics & Insights**:
  - Sales forecasting
  - Seasonal trends
  - Customer behavior analysis
- **Notifications**:
  - Low stock alerts
  - Daily sales summary
  - Payment reminders
- **Integration**:
  - Accounting software integration
  - Payment gateway integration
  - SMS gateway for receipts/alerts
- **Security**:
  - User authentication
  - Audit logs
  - Data encryption
  - Role-based permissions

## üåç African Market Specific Features

### 1. **Mobile Money Integration**
- Support for major African mobile money providers
- Quick payment processing
- Transaction verification

### 2. **Multi-Currency Support**
- Support for multiple African currencies
- Currency conversion
- Exchange rate tracking

### 3. **Local Business Practices**
- Credit sales tracking (common in African retail)
- Installment payment tracking
- Group/wholesale pricing
- Market day tracking

### 4. **Low-Resource Optimization**
- Lightweight app (works on low-end phones)
- Minimal data usage
- Battery efficient
- Works on slow internet connections

### 5. **Cultural Considerations**
- Simple, intuitive interface (low tech literacy)
- Visual icons and colors
- Voice prompts (future)
- Community/group sales support

## üì± User Personas

### Small Shop Owner (Mama/Papa Shop)
- **Needs**: Simple, fast sales tracking, inventory management
- **Tech Level**: Basic smartphone user
- **Key Features**: Quick POS, low stock alerts, simple reports

### Retail Shop Manager
- **Needs**: Detailed inventory, staff management, reports
- **Tech Level**: Moderate
- **Key Features**: Full inventory management, multi-user, analytics

### Mall/Chain Store Owner
- **Needs**: Multi-location management, consolidated reporting
- **Tech Level**: Advanced
- **Key Features**: Multi-shop, advanced analytics, integrations
